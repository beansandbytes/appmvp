Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Booking and Reservations

How do I book a parking spot?

You can book a parking spot by visiting our website and entering your flight details. Follow the prompts to select your preferred time slots and complete your reservation.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled drop-off time without any fees.

Do I need to create an account to book a parking spot?

No, creating an account is optional. However, registering can make future bookings faster and allow you to view your booking history.

Pricing and Payment

How is the pricing calculated?

Pricing is dynamic and depends on the demand for specific time slots, the duration of your parking, and any special events. You'll see the total price during the booking process.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept major credit cards and digital wallets such as Apple Pay and Google Pay.

Are there any additional fees?

There are no hidden fees. The price displayed during booking is the total amount you'll pay.
